Who I Am

Hello! My name is Kayden Pierce, and I am from Frankfort, Kentucky. I am currently taking my first web development class, and this website is part of learning how to build and structure webpages using HTML.

I work for UK Athletics, which has given me the opportunity to be around college sports and see how much organization and teamwork goes into events. Being part of that environment has made me interested in combining my passion for sports with useful technical skills like web development.

This class is helping me understand how websites are created from the ground up. I am learning that building a site is not just about how it looks, but about how information is organized so people can easily read and understand it.

My Interests

In my free time, I enjoy watching sports, playing video games, traveling, and spending time with my friends. Sports have always been a big part of my life, especially following University of Kentucky teams.

Traveling is something I enjoy because it allows me to experience new places and step away from everyday routines. Whether it is for sports events or just for fun, I like seeing different environments and meeting new people.
